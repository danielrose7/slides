How it works

Three steps. Zero data entry.

Your team keeps working the way they already do — forwarding emails. Plantiful handles the rest.

Step 01

Forward the email

When a customer order comes in — email, PDF, spreadsheet — just forward it to your Plantiful intake address.

Step 02

AI extracts & matches

AI reads every attachment — PDFs, spreadsheets, images — and matches line items to your customers and product catalog.

Step 03

Review the draft

A draft sales order appears in Plantiful — customer matched, products mapped, quantities set. Confirm and go.

Benefits

Less busywork. More peace of mind.

Order intake shouldn't mean re-typing emails into a system. Let AI handle the tedious parts so your team can focus on what matters.

Eliminate manual data entry

Fewer errors, fewer returns
